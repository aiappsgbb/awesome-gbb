## Sub-segments

- Premium / luxury (German premium OEMs, UK heritage brands, Japanese premium)
- Volume (European mass-market, Japanese / Korean global volume, US Big-Three)
- EV-pure-play (US EV pioneers, Chinese EV scale players, European EV-only spin-outs)
- Commercial-vehicle (heavy-truck and bus manufacturers)

`Vehicle`, `Recall`, `ProductionRun`, `TierOneSupplier`, `Spec`,
`Homologation`, `Battery`.
